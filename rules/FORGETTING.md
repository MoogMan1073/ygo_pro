# FORGETTING

Source: https://www.masterduelmeta.com/articles/guides/expanded-rule-book#FORGETTING

### Huh?
After a monster has been Summoned, it starts to carry all sorts of information with it, such as: 
 
 
 The time of its Summon. 
 Exactly how it was Summoned. 
 What effects are applying to it. 
 Etc. 
 When a monster stops being face-up for a while, it ' forgets ' some of that information, but remembers other parts. This can happen when it is flipped face-down by Loading... 
 or temporarily banished by Loading... 
 .

##### If a monster stops being face-up temporarily
It will forget: 
 
 
 Any properties the cards used for its Summon (Tributes, materials, etc.) had at the time of the Summon. 
 Any previously applied effects.

##### Examples:
Loading... 
 will forget whether it was Summoned by Tributing a Naturia Beast Monster. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 A monster Summoned by Loading... 
 will not be destroyed if face-down and then flipped face-up before the End Phase. 
 It will not forget: 
 
 
 Which particular cards were used for its Summon. 
 Whether it was Special Summoned or not. 
 Whether it was Summoned that turn.

##### Examples:
If a Loading... 
 is flipped face-down, then flipped face-up, and is targeted by Loading... 
 , you can Special Summon the materials. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 An Loading... 
 that is flipped face-down and then back up will still be sent to the Graveyard during the End Phase. 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Loading... 
 - or any "Yosenju" monster - set by Loading... 
 will return to the hand in the End Phase if Normal Summoned that turn. 
 Additionally, there are some variances between whether the monster was flipped face-down or temporarily banished .

##### Flipped Face-down:
It will forget: 
 
 
 Where it was Summoned from. 
 If it was Pendulum Summoned. 
 The exact method used for its Summon.

##### Examples:
If you Summon Loading... 
 , flip it face-down, and then back up again, your opponent may NOT send monsters from the Deck for Loading... 
 . 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 
 Loading... 
 Summoned by its own effect that is flipped face-down, then flipped face-up will have its original ATK of 3000. 
 It will not forget: 
 
 
 If its Battle Position has changed that turn. 
 If it attacked that turn.

##### Temporarily Banished:
It will not forget: 
 
 
 Where it was Summoned from. 
 If it was Pendulum Summoned.

##### Example:
If you Summon Loading... 
 , and temporarily banish it, your opponent MAY send monsters from the Deck for Loading... 
 when it returns to the field.
